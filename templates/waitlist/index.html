Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the launch.

When will the templates launch?
We're targeting launch in approximately 60 days. Sign up for the waitlist and we'll notify you the moment we go live — plus you'll get early access pricing.
What kind of templates will be available?
Our initial collection includes SaaS landing pages, portfolio sites, e-commerce storefronts, blog layouts, and dashboard templates. All built with the same retro-futuristic design language you see here.
How do crypto payments work?
After purchase, you'll send ETH or USDC to a generated address. Our system monitors the blockchain for confirmation (~5 minutes). Once verified, the template is unlocked in your dashboard for instant download.
Will I own the templates forever?
Absolutely. No subscriptions. No recurring fees. You pay once and retain lifetime access to download your purchased templates anytime from your Weave dashboard.
Can I modify the templates?
Yes. Every template ships with fully editable source files. Modify, customize, and adapt them to your needs. Use them for client projects or personal use.
What happens after I sign up?
You'll be added to our waitlist and receive a confirmation email. We'll send occasional updates on our progress and notify you immediately when we launch — with exclusive early-access pricing.
Can I request specific templates?
Absolutely. We're building our collection based on community input. Reply to any of our emails or reach out on social media with what you'd like to see.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes. We offer a 14-day refund policy on all template purchases. If something doesn't meet your expectations, we'll make it right or issue a full refund to your crypto wallet.
