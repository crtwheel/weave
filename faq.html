FAQ

What is Weave?
Weave sells premium Tailwind CSS landing page templates. Pay once with crypto, download the full source, and use it for any project.
How do I pay with crypto?
Send the exact amount in ETH or USDC to the wallet address on the product page. After confirming, submit your transaction hash and wallet address. You'll get access once verified.
How long does verification take?
Usually under 5 minutes. The seller verifies each transaction on Etherscan. Check your dashboard for status updates.
What if I send the wrong amount?
If you send less, it's rejected. If you send more, contact us. Always double-check before sending.
Can I get a refund?
Digital products are final. If there's a genuine issue with the template, contact us and we'll fix it.
Do I need an account?
Yes — create a free account on the Dashboard. This links purchases to your profile for download access.
