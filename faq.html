Frequently Asked Questions

What is Weave?
Weave sells premium Tailwind CSS landing page templates. Pay once with crypto, download the full source, use it for any project.
How do I pay with crypto?
Send the exact amount in ETH or USDC to the wallet address on the product page. After the transaction confirms, submit the transaction hash and your sending wallet address in the verification form. You'll get access once verified.
How long does verification take?
Usually under 5 minutes. The seller verifies each transaction on Etherscan. Status updates on your dashboard.
What if I send the wrong amount?
If you send less than the listed price, the transaction will be rejected. If you send more, contact us. Always double-check before sending.
Can I get a refund?
Digital product sales are final. If there's a genuine issue with the template, contact us and we'll fix it.
Do I need an account?
Yes — create a free account on the Dashboard page. This links purchases to your profile for download access.
What templates are coming next?
Portfolio, Startup Pre-Sale, Waitlist/Coming Soon, and Blog templates are planned.
