FAQ

Everything you need to know about Weave

What is Weave?
Weave sells premium Tailwind CSS templates. Pay once with crypto, download the full source, and use it for any project. No subscriptions, no bank accounts — just great design.
How do I pay with crypto?
Send the exact amount in ETH or USDC to the wallet address shown on the product page. Once sent, copy your transaction hash and paste it into the purchase form along with your sending wallet address. The seller verifies the transaction and you get instant access.
How long does verification take?
Under 5 minutes. The seller checks each transaction on Etherscan. You'll see the status update in your Dashboard as soon as it's verified.
What if I send the wrong amount?
If you send less than the asking price, the transaction will be rejected and you'll need to send the correct amount. If you accidentally overpay, contact us and we'll sort it out.
Can I get a refund?
Digital product sales are final. If you run into a genuine issue with the template — broken layout, missing files, etc. — reach out and we'll fix it promptly.
Do I need an account?
Yes. Creating a free account on the Dashboard lets you track purchases, download templates, and manage your library. No account means no download access.
What's coming next?
We're working on Portfolio, Startup, Waitlist, and Blog templates. Follow us for release announcements.
How do I get support?
Reach out through our Discord community or email support@weave.io. We typically respond within a few hours.
Can I upgrade my purchase to a bundle?
Yes! Contact us after your purchase and we'll help you upgrade to a bundle at a discount.
How are templates delivered?
Once your payment is verified, you can download the template ZIP directly from your Dashboard. Links are signed and expire after 1 hour for security.
